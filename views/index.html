About UpNepa Bot

#upnepa bot is a friendly bot that tells people areas where power is available.
The bot also collects data from people when light is available
To indicate power is avialable in your area just type upnepa and area e.g
[ upnepa douglas ]
To ask about power in an area just type area e.g
[ surulere ]
